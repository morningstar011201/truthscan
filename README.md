⚡ TruthScan AI 
TruthScan is an AI-powered chat analysis tool that helps you understand the real emotional intent behind conversations.

🧠 In simple words
You paste a chat (or upload a screenshot), and TruthScan tells you:
💚 How interested the other person is
🚩 If there are trust issues or possible lies
🧩 Hidden behavioral patterns (mixed signals, push-pull, etc.)
🔮 Where the relationship is heading
⚡ A clear, blunt verdict

🎯 Why it exists
Modern conversations are confusing:
late replies
mixed signals
sudden mood changes
emotional inconsistency
TruthScan helps answer:
“What does this actually mean?”

⚙️ How it works (simple idea)
It analyzes:
response timing
effort balance
emotional tone
conversation patterns
Then converts that into:
👉 structured psychological insights

👥 Who it’s for
People in dating / situationships
Anyone overthinking chats
People trying to understand intent, not just words

⚡ What makes it different
TruthScan is not a chatbot or assistant.
It’s more like:
🧠 “An emotional scanner for conversations”
Fast, direct, and designed to give clarity — not long explanations

🔥 Example
You paste a chat → it might say:
Attraction: 62%
Trust Risk: 48%
Pattern: Mixed signals
Verdict: They are interested but emotionally inconsistent
